What is ILMoS?

ILMoS is way more than a mobile application. It is a computer vision guided project which improves library and study hall experience of users, enables efficient use of libraries and minimizes abuse of desks. Data coming from the camera(s) deployed is processed by object detection algorithms, and any change in the status of the desks is reflected to the database. Our mobile application responds to those changes by updating the status of corresponding desks on the dashboard. This application is aimed to be used in the libraries and study halls; therefore, our expected users are students.

Services

Check our Services

Dashboard

We guarantee that you will not struggle to find a vacant desk in study hall. We designed a dashboard that shows the occupancy status of desks. Check it and find the place without wandering!

QR Codes

Each desk has a unique QR code and you can start your studying session by scanning it.

Profile Screen

After starting your session, you will have a profile screen that includes your session information.

Notifications

We will notify you when your study break ends and your turn in waiting queue comes.

Privacy

We do not collect any data about your identity! Our user system runs anonymous on your device. This means that we only want to know if desks are occupied or vacant. Therefore, you do not need to worry to be seen by a camera.

Queueing System

If there is no any vacant desk in study hall, you can queue up and wait your turn by using our app while getting done your other jobs or just chilling somewhere nearby.
